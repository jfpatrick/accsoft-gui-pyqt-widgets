.. note:: In Acc-Py, we are relying on `Qt Widgets <https://doc.qt.io/qt-5/qtwidgets-index.html>`__, as opposed to
          `QtQuick <https://wiki.qt.io/Introduction_to_Qt_Quick>`__. While you are free to use QtQuick
          with our
          `PyQt distribution <https://wikis.cern.ch/display/ACCPY/PyQt+distribution?src=contextnavpagetreemode>`__,
          we are not planning to ship any QtQuick widgets.
